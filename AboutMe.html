Who Am i?

My Name is Teun Weijdener:

I create creative solutions to tricky problems like: How can we traverse without gps? How can we design an engaging way of collecting trash? And is there a way to create a confetti canon that is compatible with consumable confetti?

It is in my nature to think out of the box with inside the box materials, or to put it in other words: How can we solve the problem by combining existing utilities, the result of my method is a fast, realistic and affordable product that is up to modern and future standards.

That is not to say i can't design anything new, i have designed/created multiple projects with new and unproven technology, it is just that my methodology dictates that you should not reinvent the wheel but use different size, tire grip and differently attached wheels to fit the project or use a mix of wheels and tracks so that the car has better grip on the road.

Tldr: I try to combine already existing technologies where i can and design new ones where needed.

What Am i?

Core competencies(Kernkwaliteiten in Dutch) are a list of charactaristics to describe a human being, examples may include: Perseverance, Attention to detail and Presenting. Core competencies are used to measure how capable/valuable a person is to the world, i compiled a list of core competencies which i think fit me best:

Perseverance:

A project is due tommorow and it's 22:00? Well it seems as though it is time to pull an all nighter again, although it may at first sight just appear as bad planning a group project can get tangled up in a multitude of ways and it's your perseverance that decides if you are going to get an A or an C, committing to something is something i hold in high regards.

Leadership:

In group projects i often find myself having the role of leader, something others think i am quite capable of.
